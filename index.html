Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor at Tianjin University. Before that, I received my B.S and Ph.D from Tianjin University in Jun. 2012 and Jun. 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Yanwei Pang. After finishing my Ph.D degree, I worked as a postdoc researcher at Tianjin University from Jul. 2018 to Jul. 2020. In 2019, I visited Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI), UAE and worked with Prof. Fahad Shahbaz Khan. My research interests are in Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, and Deep Learning.
